Complete study materials for the Cambridge **Test of Mathematics for University Admission** (TMUA).

### Overview

These handouts are organized by topic, covering all 15 knowledge areas tested in the TMUA. Each module includes core knowledge cards, worked examples from past papers (2016–2023), quick-solving tips, and practice exercises.

> All questions are from official past papers, with detailed analysis and syllabus mapping.
